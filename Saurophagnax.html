Saurophagnax

Saurophaganax is a genus of allosaurid dinosaur from the Morrison Formation of Late Jurassic Oklahoma, United States. Some paleontologists consider it to be a junior synonym and species of Allosaurus. Wikipedia
Length: 10 – 13 m
Mass: 3,000 – 4,500 kg
Lived: 155.7 million years ago - 145 million years ago (Kimmeridgian - Tithonian)
Scientific name: Saurophaganax (Lord of the eaters of saurians)
Extinction status: Extinct Encyclopedia of Life
Higher classification: Allosauridae



Saurophaganax was a carnivore. It lived in the Jurassic period and inhabited North America. Its fossils have been found in places such as Texas. Quick facts about Saurophaganax:
  • Existed from Kimmeridgian Age to Tithonian Age
  • Lived in a terrestrial habitat
  • Was a carnivore
  • Reproduced by laying eggs
  • Only one specimen has been found by paleontologists
