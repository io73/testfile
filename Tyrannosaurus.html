Tyrannosaurus

Tyrannosaurus is a genus of coelurosaurian theropod dinosaurs. The species Tyrannosaurus rex, often called T. rex or colloquially T-Rex, is one of the most well-represented of the large theropods. Tyrannosaurus lived throughout what is now western North America, on what was then an island continent known as Laramidia. Wikipedia
Height: 3.7 – 6.1 m
Mass: 4,500 – 14,000 kg
Eaten by: Tyrannosaurus
Speed: Tyrannosaurus rex: 27 km/h
Eats: Triceratops, Santanaraptor, Edmontosaurus, Corythosaurus, Tyrannosaurus


Tyrannosaurus rex lived in forested river valleys in North America during the late Cretaceous period.
It became extinct about 65 million years ago in the Cretaceous-Tertiary mass extinction.
